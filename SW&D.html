Social Welfare and development Committee

“The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others.”

The Social Welfare and Development Committee is a student-run organization of Vishwakarma institute of technoly pune that aims to provide a platform for students to engage in social welfare activities. The committee organizes various events and activities throughout the year to raise awareness about social issues and to encourage students to contribute to the community. The committee also works closely with local NGOs and other organizations to support their initiatives and projects. Our goal is to create a culture of social responsibility and to inspire students to make a positive impact on society.

Social services

The 'Social Services' activity aimed to cultivate responsibility and active engagement with society and the environment. Five impactful activities were conducted, providing students with real-world experience and a deeper understanding of social work principles.

The activities included:

  • Tree Plantation Drive
  • Blood Donation Camp
  • Clothes Donation Drive
  • Cleanliness Drive
  • Awareness Campaigns

Events

The Social Welfare and Development Committee organizes a variety of events throughout the year to raise awareness about social issues and to encourage students to contribute to the community. These events provide students with an opportunity to engage in social welfare activities and make a positive impact on society.

Some of the events organized by the committee include:

  • Charity Drives
  • Workshops and Seminars
  • Volunteer Programs
  • Community Service Projects
  • Awareness Campaigns

Get in touch

If you have any questions or would like to get involved with the Social Welfare and Development Committee, please feel free to contact us. We are always looking for enthusiastic students who are passionate about social welfare and community service.
